Generate good looking image/movie with VMD

The objective of this page is to give a few tips to improve the representation of molecular system using VMD. The examples that are showed have been made using the version 1.9.3 of VMD. This page contains 6 categories:

  • Before- What to do when preparing a simulation.
  • During - What to look at when the simulation is running.
  • After - What at the indication that the simulations ran smoothly, or not.

Practical example

If you want to test the tips, you can download this LAMMPS trajectory file. The trajectory file corresponds to a mixture between water and toluene, and can be openned with VMD by typing in a terminal: 

vmd dump.lammpstrj




The representation

You can change the representation of the atoms by going in Graphics, Representations, and changing the Drawing Method. My favorite representations are dynamic bonds for large molecules or graphene, and VDW for single atoms or small molecules like water. You can select to apply a representation only to a group of atoms, by choosing a group in the windows Selected Atoms. To obtain the same result as me, replace All by type 1 in the windows, it will select the oxygen of the water molecules. You can also tune the radius, and the resolution.
